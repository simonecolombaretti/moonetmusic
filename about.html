MY STORY

Moonet, aka Simone Colombaretti, is an Italian creative producer, songwriter and bass player. He took his first steps in the field of music production thanks to a cheap Dj console that his father was about to throw away, with a very basic producing software. He became aware that without learning how to play a "real" instrument and without knowing the basics of music theory he wouldn't be able to fully express himself; so he started his music studies, with a particular interest in bass guitar. Once he learned what he needed, he discovered once again his passion for music arrangement and creative production, and decided not to limitate himself just being a bass player. That's how his fist EP, OUSIA vol.1, was born: from the will of not being locked in a defined genre, but fusing together hip-hop and neo-soul vibes with sampled sounds and pitched voices, giving birth to mixed sounds and groove that will bring you in a colorful place of your own, also thanks to the instrumental tracks. In fact, OUSIA is the ancient greek word for "essence": the artist wants to express his essence but he primarily wants people to see their own trough his beats, interpreting them as freely as they desire.
